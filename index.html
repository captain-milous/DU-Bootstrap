Excursions in Prague


12 centuries of history, over 500 castles, more than 100 museums and art galleries... The first settlement at what is now Prague has been traced to the second half of the 9th century. The oldest building was Vyšehrad (hrad, “castle”), set on a commanding right-bank hill.
